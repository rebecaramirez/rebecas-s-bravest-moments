High School: Making new friends

This popular girl at school kept making fun of my friend's pixi hair cut and said things like "you look like a boy, who would ever want to date that!" I was afraid to stand up to her because I didn't want the other popular kids to make fun of me, but defending my friend was important to me, so I spoke up.

Moving to a New School

I was really scared to leave my old school and move across the country. I didn't know what the new school would be like and was worried I wouldn't be able to make new friends. Even though I was scared, I wanted to try and meet new people.

Asking for Help

My parents have been fighting a lot at home and it's been stressing me and my brother out. For a while I just kept it to myself, but it felt like I was bottling up my feelings about it. It was really hard to talk about with people, but I knew reaching out for help was better than ignoring my feelings.
